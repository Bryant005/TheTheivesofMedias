About RetroTube

Mission and accessibility

RetroTube recreates the early-2000s video sharing experience while following modern accessibility and SEO practices: clear headings, keyboard navigation, captions/transcripts, and readable contrast and font sizes.

Standards followed

  • Semantic HTML for headings and lists
  • Keyboard accessible controls and focus styles
  • Alt text for images and downloadable transcripts
  • Contrast ratios and font sizes tuned for readability
